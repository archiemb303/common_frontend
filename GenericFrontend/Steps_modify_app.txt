1. Working dir: django-admin startproject <projectname>
  e.g: django-admin startproject genericfrontend
2. Copy common folder, db.sqlite, requirements.txt.
3. Also Copy and replace <projectname>.urls and <projectname.settings>.
4.Copy dev_settings, prod_settings, shared_settings,staging_settings.
5.Press ctrl+shift+r, replace <wws> with projectname>
e.g:replace wws with genericfrontend.
